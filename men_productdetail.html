How to Measure:

  • Chest: Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape horizontal.
  • Shoulder: Measure straight across the back from shoulder seam to shoulder seam.
  • Sleeve: Measure from the top of the shoulder seam to the cuff.
  • Length: Measure from the highest point of the shoulder to the bottom hem of the shirt.

All measurements are in inches. Please allow for a tolerance of +/- 0.5 inches.
